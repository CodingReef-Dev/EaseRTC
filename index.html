Welcome To EaseRTC. This is an online Example to help you understand how powerful EaseRTC is.

To Get Started, Click on both the buttons bellow:

Follow the bellow steps to start the video confrencing

Once you enter the 'user1' file on the first box is filled with some text this is call as an SDP

Copy it and go to user2

Paste it on the first box, click on connect

Then in the user2, second box you will find another SDP, copy it and paste it in the second box in user1

Then click on connect. you will find the video being disaplyed

Hey! it works

If it dosen't please check if your device has an camera and microphone

*Before you get started. Please note that we will be needing your audio and video permissions *
